Why We Built This

Courts do not decide cases based on emotion — they decide based on facts, procedure, and applicable law.

Court AI was built to help users understand how judges reason, what questions courts ask, and where cases succeed or fail.

This system does not replace a lawyer. It mirrors legal thinking to promote clarity, preparedness, and legal literacy.
